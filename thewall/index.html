About

This is an unofficial fan-made app for checking out One Shot Gurl fan art, one piece per person. Website made by @biolithic Contact him for questions/issues about or to remove content from this app. Special thanks to One Shot Gurl and Meredith for the logos/art, Youtube for the platform and choice, and all the wonderful Snipers for their art!

The Wall Disclaimer

Images are artwork from real fans given to One Shot Gurl 2016-2018. These were taken from the One Shot Nation Discord channel, Twitter, Instagram, and One Shot Gurl's videos. "The Wall" is a reference to the actual wall One Shot Gurl posted these pictures on, which is coloured Michigan Wolverine Maize just like this virtual wall.
