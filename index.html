This is an unofficial collection of some historical items of the channel of One Shot Gurl. Discord, Twitter, Instagram of One Shot Gurl are the official sources. However, as the years go by things tend to get cluttered or could get lost. This page, therefore is an open-source backup of some of the good memories over the years.

Pax meetup photos

Photos from 2017 onwards of One Shot Gurl getting to meet her community, as well as OSN members getting together at various gaming conventions such as Pax. This is currently a private, members only photo group you may request access to.

OSN fan art

Photos from 2017 onwards of fan art created for One Shot Gurl curated from Discord, Instagram, Twitter and "Opening Fan Mail" videos.

Milestone celebration videos

For some of the channel milestones One Shot Gurl acheived such as 200,000, 250,000 and 300,000, the OSN created celebration videos for the events.

Stream Baes

A list of who was the Stream Bae for each stream for the early days of the channel.

Played With

A list of who played with One Shot Gurl on each stream for the early days of the channel.

Outcomes

A list of wins and statistics for each stream for the early days of the channel.

Stream Chat

A searchable chat archive and community analytics of most of the Youtube streams for 2018 and 2019.

Wendys

In 2017, there was a large push to get Wendy's restaurants to sponsor One Shot Gurl. Here is an example of a marketing push by OSN.

Song lyrics and sheet music

A compilation of song lyrics by DougInOrlando for One Shot Gurl and song interpretation sheet music by Biolithic to songs written by One Shot Gurl.
